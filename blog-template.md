Opening hook that draws readers in. One or two sentences max. Set up the context without being generic.

![Main Photo Alt Text](/images/blog/your-image.jpg)

The **Product Name** does [key thing]. One strong statement.

This isn't a spec sheet review. Brief context about how you tested it and what the reader will learn.

## First Major Section

Start with a strong claim or observation.

Out of the box you get:

- **Feature 1** — brief explanation
- **Feature 2** — brief explanation
- **Feature 3** — brief explanation

Short conclusion sentence for this section.

![Diagram or Detail Photo](/images/blog/detail-image.jpg)

> **Note:** Callout box for important tips or observations.

## Second Major Section

Lead with the main point.

Short supporting sentences. Keep paragraphs to 2-3 sentences max.

Follow-up thought that expands on the point.

## Third Major Section

Here's the thing about [topic]: **make the key point bold**.

When things go wrong, it's usually because of:

- Reason one
- Reason two
- Reason three

What this means in practice.
